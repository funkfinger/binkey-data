# Akro Mills Cabinet 6 - SMT Device Storage and Containers

Cabinet 6 is an Akro Mills storage cabinet with 24 bins dedicated to organizing and storing surface-mount technology (SMT) devices and components. Each bin is configured with multiple small parts containers (approximately 30 per bin) for granular organization of SMT components such as resistors, capacitors, diodes, transistors, and other small electronic parts.

## Cabinet Configuration

- **Total Bins**: 24
- **Bin Type**: Standard bins (same size as Cabinet 4)
- **Purpose**: SMT device storage with small parts containers
- **Organization**: Each bin holds ~30 small parts containers for component organization
- **Status**: Currently being populated
