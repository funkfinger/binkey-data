## Details

The MMBZ5240BLT1G is a surface-mount Zener diode from onsemi with a nominal Zener voltage of 10V. It's ideal for voltage regulation, overvoltage protection, and reference circuits in compact SMT designs.

## Description

This general-purpose Zener diode is suitable for low-power voltage regulation and protection applications. The SOT-23 package provides excellent space efficiency for modern PCB designs.

## Specifications

- **Type:** Surface Mount Zener Diode
- **Package:** SOT-23 (3-pin)
- **Zener Voltage (Vz):** 10V nominal
- **Zener Voltage Range:** 9.5V - 10.5V (typical tolerance)
- **Maximum Zener Current (Izm):** ~500mA
- **Maximum Power Dissipation:** 350mW (at 25°C)
- **Leakage Current:** Typically in the microampere range at reverse bias
- **Temperature Coefficient:** Approximately +0.06%/°C
- **Operating Temperature Range:** -55°C to +150°C
- **RoHS Compliant:** Yes (lead-free)

## Applications

- Voltage regulation circuits
- Overvoltage protection
- Reference circuits
- Transient suppression
- Power supply feedback networks
