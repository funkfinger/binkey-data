## Details

The ZR431LF02TA is a precision adjustable shunt voltage regulator from Diodes Incorporated, similar to the classic TL431 design. It provides stable voltage regulation and is commonly used in power supply feedback control circuits.

## Description

This is a high-performance adjustable shunt regulator suitable for voltage regulation, voltage reference generation, and switching power supply feedback control applications. The device offers low dynamic impedance and high stability.

## Specifications

- **Type:** Adjustable Shunt Regulator
- **Package:** SOT-23 (3-pin)
- **Reference Voltage:** Typically 2.5V (adjustable via external resistors)
- **Operating Voltage Range:** 2.5V to 36V
- **Output Current:** Up to 100mA
- **Temperature Range:** -40°C to +125°C (industrial grade)
- **Features:** Low dynamic impedance, high stability, low noise

## Applications

- Voltage regulation circuits
- Voltage reference generation
- Switching power supply feedback control
- Precision current sources
- Overvoltage protection circuits

## Storage

Store in cool, dry location. Keep in protective sleeve to prevent damage.
