## Description

The ACES MSDV-2008-AKA0T01 is a vertical through-hole microSD card connector with 8 positions and gold-plated contacts. This is a minimal design microSD socket suitable for PCB mounting in applications requiring microSD card storage. The vertical orientation makes it ideal for space-constrained designs where the card sits perpendicular to the PCB.

## Circuit Integration

### Basic Connection

The microSD connector connects directly to microSD cards with standard pinout:
- Pin 1: Chip Select (CS)
- Pin 2: MOSI (Master Out Slave In)
- Pin 3: VSS (Ground)
- Pin 4: VDD (Power)
- Pin 5: Clock (CLK)
- Pin 6: VSS (Ground)
- Pin 7: MISO (Master In Slave Out)
- Pin 8: Reserved/NC

### Typical Application

Connect to microcontroller SPI interface for SD card communication:
- CS to GPIO pin
- MOSI to SPI MOSI
- MISO to SPI MISO
- CLK to SPI CLK
- VDD to 3.3V power
- VSS to ground
