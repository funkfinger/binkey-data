## Details

1kΩ SMT resistors are one of the most commonly used resistor values, ideal for pull-up/pull-down networks, general biasing, and signal conditioning.

## Specifications

- **Resistance**: 1kΩ (1000Ω)
- **Tolerance**: ±5% or ±1%
- **Package**: SMT (0603, 0805, or 1206)
- **Power Rating**: 0.1W typical
- **Temperature Coefficient**: ±100-200 ppm/°C
- **Operating Temperature**: -55°C to +125°C

## Applications

- Pull-up and pull-down networks (most common)
- General purpose biasing
- Signal conditioning
- Voltage dividers
- Microcontroller I/O protection
- Sensor signal conditioning
