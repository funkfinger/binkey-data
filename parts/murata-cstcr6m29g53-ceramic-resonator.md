## Details

The Murata CSTCR6M29G53-R0 is a ceramic resonator from the CERALOCK® series designed for frequency control and timing applications. It features a built-in 15pF capacitor, eliminating the need for external load capacitors in many applications. This SMD component is ideal for microcontroller clock circuits and timing applications.

## Description

The CSTCR ceramic resonator is a piezoelectric ceramic device that vibrates at a specific frequency when excited. The built-in capacitor provides the necessary load capacitance for stable oscillation. These resonators are commonly used as clock sources for microcontrollers, microprocessors, and other digital circuits where precise timing is required.
