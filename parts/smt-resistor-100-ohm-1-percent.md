## Details

100Ω 1% precision SMT resistors in cut tape format. These precision resistors are ideal for applications requiring tighter tolerance specifications compared to standard 5% resistors.

## Description

These are high-precision surface-mount resistors with 1% tolerance, suitable for precision circuits, measurement equipment, and applications where accurate resistance values are critical. The cut tape format provides convenient storage and easy component access.

## Specifications

- **Resistance Value:** 100Ω
- **Tolerance:** ±1%
- **Package:** SMT (0603, 0805, or 1206)
- **Temperature Coefficient:** Typically ±100 ppm/°C
- **Power Rating:** Depends on package size (typically 0.1W for 0603, 0.25W for 0805, 0.5W for 1206)
- **Operating Temperature Range:** -55°C to +125°C
- **Format:** Cut tape in plastic sleeves

## Applications

- Precision measurement circuits
- Instrumentation and test equipment
- Audio circuits requiring accurate impedance matching
- Sensor signal conditioning
- Reference circuits
- Current limiting in precision applications
- Feedback networks in precision amplifiers

## Storage

Store in cool, dry location. Keep in protective sleeves to prevent damage and maintain organization.

## Notes

These 1% precision resistors are significantly more accurate than standard 5% resistors and are suitable for applications where component tolerance is critical to circuit performance.
