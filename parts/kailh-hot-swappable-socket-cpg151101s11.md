## Details

The Kailh CPG151101S11 is a hot-swappable PCB socket designed for mechanical keyboard switches. These sockets allow users to easily swap out mechanical switches without soldering, making keyboard customization and maintenance simple and tool-free. The socket is compatible with Kailh mechanical switches and is widely used in DIY mechanical keyboard projects.

## Description

Hot-swappable sockets are essential components for modern mechanical keyboards that support switch replacement without soldering. The Kailh CPG151101S11 socket provides a reliable connection point for mechanical switches on PCBs. Each socket accommodates a standard mechanical switch stem and provides secure retention while allowing easy insertion and removal of switches.

## Installation

Hot-swappable sockets are typically soldered to the keyboard PCB during manufacturing. Once installed, switches can be inserted and removed by hand without tools. Simply push the switch into the socket until it clicks into place, and pull straight up to remove.
