- **Reverse Breakdown Voltage (VR)**: 75V-100V (typical)

- **Switching Time**: <4ns (typical for high-speed variants)

## Circuit Integration

### Basic Rectification Circuit

Connect for signal rectification:
- Anode to signal source
- Cathode to load or ground
- Provides one-way current flow

### Clamp Diode Application

Protect circuits from voltage spikes:
- Cathode to positive supply
- Anode to signal line
- Provides protection against negative transients

### Logic Circuit Protection

Protect microcontroller I/O:
- Cathode to positive supply
- Anode to I/O pin
- Prevents overvoltage damage

- **1N4148W**: Classic high-speed switching diode
