## Details

The MCP73831T-2ACI-OT is a single-cell linear lithium-ion/lithium-polymer battery charge management controller from Microchip Technology. It provides a complete linear charging solution with integrated power MOSFETs and comprehensive protection circuitry.

## Description

This compact battery charger IC is designed for simple, reliable charging of single-cell lithium batteries. It features integrated power MOSFETs, thermal regulation, and overvoltage fault protection for safe battery charging.

## Specifications

- **Type:** Linear Li-Ion/LiPo Battery Charger
- **Package:** SOT-23-5 (5-pin)
- **Charge Current (Max):** 500mA
- **Battery Float Voltage:** 4.2V ±1%
- **Input Supply Voltage:** 3.75V to 6V (absolute max 6.5V)
- **Battery Type:** Single-cell Li-Ion or Li-Polymer
- **Thermal Regulation:** Protects device up to 120°C junction temperature
- **Charge Status Output:** Open-drain CHRG pin (pulls low during charging)
- **Fault Protection:** Overvoltage protection included
- **Features:**
  - Integrated power MOSFETs
  - Thermal shutdown
  - Overvoltage fault protection
  - Low external component count

## Pin Functions

- **GND:** Ground reference
- **V_IN:** Power supply input (e.g., 5V USB)
- **BAT:** Connects to battery positive terminal
- **CHRG:** Charge status indicator output (open-drain)

- USB-powered battery charging
- Portable electronics
